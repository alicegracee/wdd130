Overview

Purpose

Our water rafting company offers for all ages and all abilities water rafting fun. Where as a family, alone, or a group of friends can enjoy water rafting in our resort. Learn some techniques of rafting and enjoy your day on the river as well as exploring the area around you.

Audience

1. Who are the target customers?

Target costumers are Male Urbonites and Families with kids.

Male urbonites mainly age 18-25, single and graduate professionals. They like sports and socialising with friends. They are very active participants who would be interested in different sport activities.
Source: https://segments.sportengland.org/pdf/penPortrait-1.pdf

Families with kids. Their age range from 33-45. Part time workers or full time workers. Also, stay at home mums with kids (married). They work in call centres, salesmens and etc. They lead active life style with children.Families like sport and doing different activities, such as aerobic classes, swimming and etc. These people would be interested in different activities.
Source: https://segments.sportengland.org/pdf/penPortrait-8.pdf

2. What do they want?

They are interested in finding different activities. They are very active group of people who are looking to spend time with families and friends their free time at different venues and trying new sports, as well learn new things.

3.What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

They are looking to find more information on how you can book for attending sport activity. Interested to know prices, dates and work hours. People want to know what they can do at the resort besides rafting. Is there any age restrictions and what families with small children are able to do inside resort. Active young professionals looking how you can do a group booking and if there any discounts for families with more children and blogger groups.
